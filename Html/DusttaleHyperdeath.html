Asriel (Hyperdeath)

Protagonist / Deification

Story

In Dusttale: Hyperdeath, Asriel Dreemurr/Flowey ended up going insane after so many resets, and he decides to create his own genocide route, killing all the monsters. Dusttale: Hyperdeath takes place after the events of Dusttale and Dustbelief, where Sans and Papyrus died trying to stop the human, and Flowey went insane after so many resets. He started killing monsters to gain enough power to deal with strong monsters like Undyne, Sans, and Asgore, in order to force Asgore to tell him where the six human souls are, which he needs to absorb to become a god. The souls then merge with Asriel's soul, making the transformation permanent. He has everything he needs to stop the human. After that,he kills the human, and absorb the human's soul; with his complete form, he can reign and control timelines. He revives the monsters who died in the end, becoming a godlike observer, merely watching his reality and even others without interacting; he just observes..

Abilities

  • Soul Absorption: Consumes souls to increase power and endurance.
  • Time-Scope: Limited time manipulation to observe and influence timelines.
  • Hypergoner II: Hypergoner like the original, but intensified.
  • Chaos Buster II: Chaos Buster like the original, but intensified.
  • Shocker Breaker III: Combination of Shocker Breakers I and II, also intensified by determination.
  • Universalis Blazing: Galacta Blazing do jogo original intensificado, com a força do universo.
  • Space-Scope: Manipulação espacial limitada para observar e influenciar diversas coisas.
