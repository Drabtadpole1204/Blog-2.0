Story

Literally the Freddy 64 gang from TPRR,but I made my own version of them,and added a new character,Daemon. Freddy and Friends are a group of characters that are like the ones from the original FNAF, but in the 64 style. I will only talk about Freddy 64 and Daemon in this wiki.

Abilities

  • Toon Force: He can summon things out of nowhere like in an RPG, but this ability can only be used to summon weapons he can hold in his hand, and they can't be too big, and it's also normal Toon Force.
  • Platformer Movement: Technically, this isn't a skill, but rather something passive of his, I just added it here so it wouldn't look too empty.
  • Unique Cooking: Is also kinda of passive, can cook foods that can give magical abilities, for example.
  • Now about his abilities during the time when the Apocalypse happens: Fun Fact: By Apocalypse, I meant an infection that occurred at some point, I'm not sure if it was worldwide or just where he was, I didn't think about it, but this infection also affected Animatronics.
  • Summon Portals: He can summon portals to travel through the multiverse of FNAF and TPRR, and Roblox, which is technically a Complex Multiverse, or a Hyperverse, I'm not sure.
  • Now the equipments
  • Crescendo: Forged with blazing flames and a demonic eye. Crescendo, the Soul Stealer, rises from his ancient slumber with a shrill tone. He grabbed this sword when he was in UCN. This weapon can awaken Crescendo, making it float..

  • Redcliff Duality: Redcliff Sword and Shield, a Roblox clan at war against Korblox, he got this weapon after also ending up in Redcliff by accident. This weapon can summon a spirit, or a stand, from Redcliff.
  • Remington 870: A Remington 870.
  • Axe: A common axe.
  • Replica of Roblox's Excalibur: Self-exaplanatory, no?
  • Flashlight: Flashlight.
  • The following habilities belong to Posessed 64:
  • Ghost State: You can become translucent and pass through walls and solid objects, but you cannot attack in this state, only pass through solid things.
  • Fly: Can fly or float.
  • Anti-Visibility: He can be visible to just one person or anyone he wants, or to no one at all; another name for this ability could be Invisibility.
  • Toon Force: It's like a toon force, but much weaker.
  • Platformer Movement: He can do this as normal 64.
  • Although he has control over Body 64, he cannot cook like him, because Body 64's cooking requires unique knowledge. And creativity.
