Creator / Builder

Story

Builder64, The Architect.

Abilities

  • Builder Hammer: Used for combat, the hammer, called Aedifex only obeys Builder64. The hammer is also used to create things, like worlds.
  • Tesis: Can create things.
  • Nigh-Omnipotence: He is Nigh-Omnipotent.

AU

Builder64 is the architect of worlds, forging them and their destinies. He cannot forge the destinies of beings above him, as they are beyond the scope of his abilities. His form is based on Mario 64, and if he were to be killed, it would leave Builder64 without a form. His name is Builder, but he added 64 to it to match his form.
