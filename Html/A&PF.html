Dr.Fade

Antagonist / Scientist and Agent

Story

Dr.Fade is an extremely intelligent individual who works at 'Anomaly & Phobia Facility', a company that specializes on capturing Phobias, who take form of animatronic from Freddy's Pizzaria, and Anomalies, who are beings that shouldn't exist or aren't from that universe.

Dr.Fade specializes in capturing Anomalies and Phobias, and studying them too.

Fun-Fact: He's an OC that I created on roblox.

Abilities

  • Equipment:
  • Agony Cannon: Its a cannon that paralyzes Phobias, making them feel extreme agony.
  • Mini-Cubes: A cube used to capture/seal Phobias and Anomalies, no matter which, inside the cube, there is a pocket dimension.
  • Mask: His mask protects him from any Phobia effect or effects Anomalies can cause.
  • Teleport pad: He can teleport instantly using the mini-platform.
  • Super-Armor: Armor he can use to protect himself from almost anything on the universe. Like Water Pressure for example.
  • Multi-Tech: One of his bests inventions yet, it lets him clone things and even fly.
