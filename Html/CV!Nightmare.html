CV!Nightmare

Concept / Negativity

Story

CV!Nightmare is the opposite of CV!Dream: it represents negativity and at the same time is a protector of its own concept. It has strong ties with its twin brother and acts according to its own code. CV!Nightmare also affects the deities..

Abilities

  • Shadow Influence: Can shape negativity to control spaces, and corrupt souls using its aura. Except Dream's
  • Corrupting Touch: Temporarily weakens opponents' resistance.
  • Tentacles: Can summon tentacles from the ground and walls as well as the tentacles on its back.
  • Omni-vision: Can see all the other Multiverses, these being infinite.
  • Body Manipulation: You can manipulate your body, in which it is liquid negativity, to turn into a giant, for example, among other things.
  • Orbs: They can create orbs, which can trap or serve as pocket dimensions; these orbs are yellow.
  • Dark Manipulation: Can manipulate darkness for defense, attack, and communication.
  • Polar Immortality: He is immortal, cannot be hurt, and cannot be killed, only by Dream, because they are polar opposites. Nightmare's soul cannot be touched by others; it is impossible, except by Dream.

AU

Nightmare from Creatorverse, from my AU based on Dreamtale. Anyway, Nightmare is a character I created based on the Dreamtale Nightmare, but he has some differences, like being yellow, for example. He’s not exactly a villain or a hero, he just acts as he wants, although he is the concept and the god of negativity, as well as the guardian of the concept. He has affection for CV!Dream (Creatorverse Dream), his twin brother. Despite this, he is still a bit chaotic and knows how to be serious when necessary... sometimes too much.
