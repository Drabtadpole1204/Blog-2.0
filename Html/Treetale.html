Sansolotl

Skeleton Axolotl/ Lazy

Story

My first Undertale AU. Treetale. Fun fact: This Sans is the first character I created for this AU. Fun fact 2: His drawing looks bad because I made it on Photopea, I don't know how to use it THAT well. On paper, his head has a shape that resembles a circle stretched sideways, he has a smile and bigger eyes, he is a Skeleton Axolotl. I specifically chose this because Axolotls are known for their healing factor, and the fandom says Sans has 1 HP, but I'm not sure. Treetale is Undertale but the Monsters ressembles more like animals than in the actual game,some didnt change,though.

Abilities

  • Blue Soul: Its Telekinesis.
  • Axol Blasters: Like normal blasters, though, they have 'axolotl antennas or ears'.
  • Water Bones: Water bones, it's kind of self-explanatory.
  • Blue Bones: Blue bones, which force you to stay still so you don't get hurt when they pass by you.
  • Orange Bones: Orange bones, in which you are forced to move so you don't get hurt when they pass by you.
  • Regeneration: He can regenerate his limbs, no matter which one, except for his head, but he can survive without it because he is a skeleton.
  • Water Axol Blasters: Literally the Axol Blasters but made of water and more powerful.
  • Water Manipulation: Self-explanatory.
  • Aquatic Breathing: Self-explanatory too.
  • Space Breathing: Self-explanatory.
  • Spinning Bone: Long bones that will spin while forming an X.
  • Water Spinning Bone: Long bones that will spin while forming an X, but made of water, and stronger, and faster.

AU (Alternate Universe)

He belongs to Treetale, is kind of lazy, has a brother and a father, lives in Snowdin with them, there's not much else to say. In this AU, the three main Undertale routes still exist: Pacifist, Neutral, and Genocide.
