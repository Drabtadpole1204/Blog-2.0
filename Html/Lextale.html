Lex

Protagonist / Hybrid

Story

Lex is a hybrid between a dragon and a human who aims to be the Royal Guard, and eventually a Hero of his world.

Abilities

  • Blue Soul: Literally Telekinesis.
  • Fire Balls: Self-Explanatory, think of it as Toriel's Fire balls but a bit bigger and stronger.
  • Fire Magic: Self-Explanatory.
  • Ice Magic: Self-explanatory.
  • Martial Arts: Self-explanatory.
  • Void Magic: Lex posesses magic similar to Gaster's, he can 'materialize' bits of the Void and turn them into attacks or weapons.He hasn't mastered Void Magic yet.

AU

Lex is a hybrid between a dragon and a human, he wants to become a Royal Guard and Hero of his world, by getting into the Hero Academy. By the way, in his AU, monsters have gone to The Surface thanks to Frisk, Monsters made peace with Humans, some Monsters have decided to stay in The Underground.

Here, the Royal Guard are a special forces above the FBI and any military in the past, people need to go through the Hero Academy fist to get to the Royal Guard, which is Lex's dream.

Lextale has a timeskip, and was another of my first AUs, alongside Treetale and Invader, all of these mentioned are Undertale AUs.
