Murderverse

Villain Protagonist/ Assassin

Story

Like in Dusttale, he remembers the human's genocidal routes and does the same things as in Dusttale: he faces and kills the human, absorbing their soul. He commits his own genocidal route on the surface. The amount of hatred and determination that Murdervese had was overwhelming, and to avoid dying, he let Hatred and Determination modify his body. The red part is Determination, while the black part is Hatred, and his purple-pink aura is Perseverance. He no longer feels anything besides this and the desire for destruction for power.

Abilities

  • Hate Discharge: Lets out a scream that deals damage and makes his attacks stronger.
  • SAVE: Can create a SAVE and reload it, but this only affects his universe, or universes that he has conquered.
  • ERASE: Can erase things/people from existence, does not affect what cannot be erased or what is above it.
  • Murder Blasters: Summons blasters where half of the blaster is black/hate, and half is red/determination.
  • Blue Bone: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Bone: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, move.
  • Spinning Bone: Long bones that will spin as they form an X, one of the bones is black, meaning it is made of hatred, while the other is red, meaning it is made of determination.
