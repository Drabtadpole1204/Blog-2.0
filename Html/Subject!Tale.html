Teacher!Sans

Math Teacher/Side Character

Story

Subject!Tale is the AU where Sans is a math teacher. The underground of this AU has several schools, and education influences the power of souls. There are two main routes when Frisk falls: Pacifist (education, reconciliation) and Genocide (conflict and combat).

Abilities

  • Math Time: Activates a mode that boosts stats and changes attacks with mathematical symbols, can stay in form depending on his energy.
  • Equation Strike: Attacks with calculated properties that vary depending on the target.
  • Remote Bones: Bones that will follow the target.
  • Math Blaster A Blaster that shoots energy and bullets; if hit by the energy, a mini-game with 5 math expressions will start, and each mistake will make Sans stronger.
  • Blue Bones: ASelf-explanatory, to avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Bones: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, move.
  • Spinning Bone: Long bones that will spin while forming an X.
  • Math Spinning Bone: Long bones that will spin while forming an X, and send you to the mini-game of 5 math problems when touched.

AU

Subject! Tale focuses on underground schools; Asgore promotes education while hiding the need for six human souls to allow the monsters to leave. Although lazy, Sans knows how to be serious, he is very calm and makes jokes, he is the math teacher, he helps the students, on the Genocide Route, he will try to stop Frisk at all costs, one of his attacks involves calculations. Math Time is the 'mode' that Teacher!Sans enters when he is nearly hit on the Genocide Route, his eye turns into a division symbol, and his STATS increase.
