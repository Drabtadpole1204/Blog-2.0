Bakon

Antagonist / Mad Man

Story

In BloodBlox Bakon went insane after a lot of Genocide routes and decided to do his own to stop the Craftian.

Abilities

  • Blue Soul: Literally Telekinesis
  • Bones: Bones
  • Blasters: Like the normal Blasters
  • Blue Bones: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Bones: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, move.
  • Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X.
  • Blue Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X. To avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X., to avoid damage, move.

AU

A long time ago, two races ruled over the Earth, Bloxians and Craftians. One day, war broke between the 2 races, the Bloxians were defeated and sealed underground, they to this day wait for the 7th Craftian soul, needed to break The Barrier Block.

...The 7th Craftian had the power of RESET, and abused it to entertain himself, make friends 3 times, and do 365 Genocides, and 2 Neutral Routes.

...But this time....Something New happens.....

Fun facts: Craftian is a reference to Minecraft, and Bloxians are a reference to Roblox
