Glass Sans

Fighter / Joke Character

Story

Glass!Sans, or Glass, is a meme/joke character from my Roblox game 'Sans Battles!' along with Glitch!Sans, or Glitch.

Abilities

  • Glass Spinning Bone: Long Bones that Form an X when they spin, but they also explode and send out shards after being used.
  • Glass Bone: Throws glass bones that explode and scatter shards on contact.
  • Glass Gaster Blaster: Like the original Blasters, but made of glass, and these explode, sending glass shards after using their energy beam.
  • Dodges: He has 500 dodges.
  • Triple Big Gaster Blaster: 3 Big Gaster Blasters.
  • Speed Coil: He gets faster.
  • Super Speed Coil: He gets WAY faster.
