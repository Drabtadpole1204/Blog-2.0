UBP Sans

Side Character and Antagonist / Neutral Route

Story

In Undertale: Breaking Point There were only Neutral Routes in EVERY Reset, and a certain skeleton has lost their patience...

Abilities

  • Blue Soul: Literally Telekinesis
  • Bones: Bones
  • Blasters: Like the normal Blasters
  • Blue Bones: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Bones: Self-explanatory, to avoid damage, move.
  • Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X.
  • Blue Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X. To avoid damage, stay still.
  • Orange Spinning Bones: Long bones that will spin while forming an X., to avoid damage, move.
