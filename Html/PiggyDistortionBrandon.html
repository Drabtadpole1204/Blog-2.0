Piggy Distortion — Brandon

Infected Brandon

Brandon

Infected / Antagonist

Story

Brandon is the most powerful infected enemy in Piggy Distortion — an entity spreading infection and causing chaos across the multiverse. Under the command of T.I.O.

Abilities

  • Immunity: Immune to any type of magical attacks.
  • Super-Strength: Although self-explanatory, his super strength is a bit different. One example of this is that he lifted Cosmo's fist, a deity whose fist is a little larger than half of the Earth, and he has already been able to reset the multiverse with his fists, and lifted an island with his right hand.
  • Interfictional Travel: He can travel to different fictions, like when he went to Undertale and corrupted Frisk and their saves.
  • Super-Speed: It has reached 50% of the speed of light once, and it has reached the cosmic horizon of the Multiverse#121 (Multiverse where Piggy Distortion is), when it was chasing someone, who was VERY fast, not the Flash,though.
  • Manipulation: It can create illusions and hallucinations.
  • Insolence: Like the other Infected Overlords from my universe, he can transform his body into a black liquid called Insolence.
  • Blood Manipulation: Self-explanatory, it just doesn't work on those who are immune.
  • Weakness:
  • Sealing Magic: The only magical attack to which he is not immune, although the sealing is temporary, it might be enough, or maybe not... constantly using this magic/spamming it will make the sealing time shorter because he will develop immunity to it.
