Geo

Pixel-made being / Side Character-Antagonist

Story

A being made out of pixels around the multiverse, he doesnt know his purpose, he started as a helpful friend and vigilante, but fate didnt have a nice future in store for him, as he becomes an antagonist later on.

Fun-Fact: He's an OC that I created on roblox.

Abilities

  • Pixel Manipulation: Pixels are technically Geo's atoms, or matter, Pixels are little cubes almost Invisible to the naked eye, they are also a force, they could be considered a force of nature, not from universes, but the Creator himself.
  • Body Manipulation: He can manipulate his body.
  • Teleportation: He can teleport instantly.
  • Pixel Force: He can boost himself, like a super form.
  • Pixelated Beam: A red beam made out of Pixels.
  • Passive Abilities:
  • Super-Endurance: Self-Explanatory.
  • Super-Durability: Self-Explanatory.

More

Geo's story started as he was created/made by Pixels that were around the multiverse, that ended up united to form him.

Geo was once a nice friend, a helpful friend and vigilante aswell but he later ends up becoming a villain as he was trying to find his purpose, which was making everything better, but he lost himself, thinking the only way to ensure peace was to control everything.
